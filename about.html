I've spent seven years running growth inside venture-backed companies — most recently as Head of Growth at a Series B fintech, before that as the third employee at a B2B SaaS that exited in 2023.

CGE is what I do now. One client at a time, the duration that fits the work. The work is the same things I did in-house — paid acquisition, the analytics layer, conversion engineering, the lifecycle automation — except the deliverable is a system your team can run when I'm gone.

I'm a single-operator practice by design. No retainers padded with busywork. No twelve-person decks. No alignment workshops. Just a working growth engine, hand-built, instrumented, and handed over.
