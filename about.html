I trained as an engineer at the University of Cape Town, then spent the last eight years in marketing — most of it deep in performance: Google Ads, conversion tracking, and the data integrity that makes both worth trusting.

I've held fractional CMO roles at notable companies across the Middle East, taken local brands national, built online stores and the systems behind them, and tripled the fleet for a luxury-transport operator in Europe. CGE is where I do that work now — for a small number of clients at a time.

CGE is a founder-led practice by design. No retainers padded with busywork, no twelve-person decks, no alignment theatre — just a working growth engine, hand-built, instrumented, and handed to your team.
